Company Background

Agentica AI is an emerging leader in delivering AI-driven business automation solutions. Founded by Moudy, the company focuses on boosting operational efficiency through domain-trained AI agents. Agentica AI’s flagship services include the Intelligence Hub™, AI Agents, a powerful Reasoning Engine, and Dynamic Workflows, which collectively enable a 10x increase in productivity while ensuring enterprise-grade security and seamless integration with leading tools.

With a strong commitment to innovation, Agentica AI addresses key challenges such as talent shortages, human error-induced security breaches, and the need for scalable, cloud-based infrastructures. The company is dedicated to transforming traditional business processes into agile, automated workflows.

Role Overview

The Chief Technology Officer (CTO) at Agentica AI will report directly to the CEO and serve as the foremost technology visionary for the company. This role demands a strategic leader who can drive the continued success of our AI-driven products and services while ensuring robust technological frameworks are in place. The CTO will spearhead the product development organization, overseeing R&D, engineering, QA, and product operations, aimed at revolutionizing the way businesses harness AI.

Key Priorities

  • Rationalization: Standardize technology platforms across Agentica AI to facilitate uniformity and ease integration of new acquisitions.
  • Team Building: Consolidate and mentor a high-performing technology team, aligning recent hires and acquisitions under one dynamic vision.

Candidate Profile

Professional Background

The ideal candidate will be a seasoned technology leader with extensive experience in enterprise software, SaaS, and cloud-based platforms. They should have a track record of driving innovation and operational efficiency in high-growth environments, specifically within companies leveraging AI and automation.

Key Attributes

  • Technical and Functional Excellence: Proven expertise across the technology stack with hands-on experience in designing and deploying scalable AI solutions.
  • Results Orientation: Demonstrated ability to ship quality products, manage complex projects, and exceed expectations in fast-paced, dynamic settings.
  • Collaboration and Influence: Strong collaborative skills to work across multifaceted teams and influence stakeholders at all organizational levels.
  • Team Building: Ability to motivate, mentor, and manage distributed technology teams ensuring alignment with company objectives.
