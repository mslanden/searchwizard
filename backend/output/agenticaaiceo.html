The Role

The new CEO will report directly to the board and will be responsible for setting the strategic direction of the company and overseeing all operations and business activities. They will ensure that the company has a clear and actionable strategic plan in place and will work closely with the team to ensure this plan is executed effectively. The overall responsibility will be for growing the brand and ensuring its continuation as a leader in the industry.

Key Priorities

  • Provide strategic leadership to the company.
  • Drive performance across all areas of the company.
  • Develop and direct the implementation of the strategic plan.
  • Oversee all operations and business activities to ensure they meet the company's objectives and are consistent with the company's overall strategy.
  • Make high-level decisions about company policy and strategy.

Candidate Profile

Professional Background

The ideal candidate for this position will be a seasoned CEO with significant experience managing an enterprise AI or software company. They will have a strong understanding of business strategies in the tech field and will be capable of building relationships with top investors and partners.   • Strong understanding of technology and AI innovations.
